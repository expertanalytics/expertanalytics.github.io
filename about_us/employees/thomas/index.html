Thomas Golding

  • thomas@xal.no

Thomas is interested in bringing to life the potential of data for automation and insight purposes. He will happily engage in the various steps of the process, from analysis and machine learning to data collection and software development.

Thomas holds a PhD in astrophysics for developing and studying models of the solar atmosphere. After finishing the PhD he worked as an analyst in the public sector. Here he took part in the business intelligence process as well as data analyses, data modeling and system architecting. Thomas also completed the two year leadership development program Teach First Norway.

The varied experience makes Thomas a diverse problem solver with a critical and systematic approach. He has the ability of quickly adopting to new projects and suggest novel solutions.
